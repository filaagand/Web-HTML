Mark Lee’s Solo Journey: A New Chapter Begins with ‘The Firstfruit’

Mark Lee

Mark Lee, a member of the globally renowned group NCT, is set to make his highly anticipated solo debut with the release of his first full-length album, "The Firstfruit," on April 7, 2025. This album marks a significant milestone in his career, as fans have long awaited the opportunity to see him fully express his artistry as a soloist. Originally scheduled for a February release, the album was delayed to allow for further refinement, ensuring that it meets the high expectations of both Mark and his fans.

With a total of 13 tracks, "The Firstfruit" promises to showcase Mark's versatility as a musician. Known for his dynamic rap skills, smooth vocals, and profound lyricism, he has played a crucial role in shaping NCT's unique sound. The album will feature a blend of genres, from R&B to hip-hop and alternative pop, highlighting his diverse musical influences. Mark himself has been deeply involved in the songwriting and production process, making this album a true reflection of his creative vision.

Before this official solo debut, Mark had previously released solo tracks like "Child" in 2022 and "200" in 2024, both of which were met with widespread acclaim. However, "The Firstfruit" marks a new chapter, as it represents his full-fledged emergence as an independent artist beyond NCT’s collaborative framework. Fans and music enthusiasts alike are eager to witness how Mark will carve out his own identity in the music industry.

The anticipation for the album has been building steadily, with teaser images and concept trailers hinting at an introspective and emotionally resonant theme. The title, "The Firstfruit," suggests a personal and meaningful debut, possibly symbolizing the culmination of his growth and experiences in the industry.

With his natural charisma and exceptional talent, Mark Lee is poised to make a significant impact with his solo venture. Whether you are a longtime fan or a new listener, "The Firstfruit" is set to be an album that leaves a lasting impression, further solidifying Mark’s status as one of the most talented and multifaceted artists of his generation.
